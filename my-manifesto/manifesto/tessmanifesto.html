My Manifesto

This is your life. Do what you Love, and do it often. If you don’t like something, change it. Life is short. If you don’t like your job, quit. If you don’t have enough time, stop watching TV. some opportunities only come once, seize them. If you are looking for the love of your life, stop; They will be waiting for you when you start doing things you love. Stop over analyzing, life is simple. All emotions are beautiful. When you eat, appreciate every last bite. Lions do not lose sleep over the opinions of sheep. Open your mind, arms, and heart to new things and people, we are united in our differences. Life is about the people you meet, and the things you create with them so go out and get stared creating. Travel often; getting lost will help you find yourself. If it does not nourish your soul, get rid of it. The world needs you. Happiness is a choice. Forgive yourself ‐ Be kinder to yourself. Nothing works unless you do. Show Gratitude. Your kindness has a ripple effect. Never apologize for being your unique self. People will never forget how you have made them feel. Authenticity and confidence are sexy. Share your story and your gifts. Be adaptable. Stay curious. Never stop learning and exploring. What you seek is seeking you. Live your dream and share your passion. YOU DON'T NEED THEIR APPROVAL!
